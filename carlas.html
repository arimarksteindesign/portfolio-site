Main Logo
The client approached me with a request to design a logo for their new pizzeria in Wayne, PA. They wanted a logo that conveyed a classic, timeless appeal, with the use of the color red. The logo was intended to be used on various marketing materials, including signage, menu designs, and online platforms.
To meet the client's requirements, I conceptualized a logo that exuded a classic pizzeria aesthetic. The logo incorporates a dominant red color to evoke a sense of warmth and appetite appeal. The font chosen for the pizzeria's name has a traditional yet modern touch, giving the logo a familiar yet contemporary feel.
