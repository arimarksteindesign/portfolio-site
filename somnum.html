The goal of this self initiated project was to create a brand identity for the mattress company, Som-Num (meaning “sleep” in Latin), that would reflect the company's commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability. The brand identity needed to be visually appealing and memorable, and it needed to communicate the company's unique value proposition.
The design approach for the Som-Num brand identity was to create a modern, sophisticated look that would appeal to a wide range of customers. The logo was designed using a clean, sans-serif font and a calming color palette. The brand mark is simple, yet elegant, which conveys the company's focus on sleep and comfort.
