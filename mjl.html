Coworking Space
For this project, I was approached by a client in search of a captivating logo for their creative workspace that exuded the spirit of women empowerment. Their vision was a logo that spoke to the strength and unity of women, celebrating their creativity and fostering a sense of community.
I spent time delving into the nuances of color psychology and the symbolism of shapes to bring their vision to life. The logo's color palette was meticulously chosen to convey a sense of empowerment and energy. The final logo is a reflection of the client's vision, through the use of color and symbolism.
