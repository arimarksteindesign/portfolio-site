Brew Lab
Café/Coffee Shop
This brand identity package was created for Brew Lab, a café that focuses on the art of brewing coffee, along with fresh baked goods and snacks. This design embodies Brew Lab's ethos through a fusion of contemporary flair and timeless coffee culture. With carefully curated fonts, a simple and recognizable logo, and bold typography, the aesthetic exudes a sense of dynamism and sophistication, inviting visitors to immerse themselves in the café's modern yet approachable ambiance. From branding collateral to digital assets, every element reflects a commitment to excellence and a passion for the craft of coffee.
